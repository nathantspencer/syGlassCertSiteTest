syGlass Certificate Verification

What are syGlass certificates?

syGlass certificates are issued to individuals who, using syGlass software, reach specific training milestones or contribute to certain participatory science collaborations. Certificates are marked according to the achievement(s) for which they were issued.

How can I verify a certificate?

Each individual to whom a certificate has been issued is assigned a certificate ID that begins with "SY" and ends in a string of numbers. Certificates can be verified by entering the certificate ID in the text entry field beneath the header "Verify a certificate" below and pressing "Verify."
